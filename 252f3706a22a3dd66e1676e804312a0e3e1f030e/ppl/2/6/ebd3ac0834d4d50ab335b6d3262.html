Narrative

Agatha (also known in records as Agata and Agnes) was born in Poland in 1872. She immigrated to the United States and lived most of her life in Plains, Pennslyvania at 110 W. Carey Street.

Her father or mother's name is unknown (as of this entry) and both entries were written as "unknown" on her death certificate.
