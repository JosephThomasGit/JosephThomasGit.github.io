Naberezny, John

Birth Name Naberezny, John
Gender male
Age at Death 73 years, 4 months, 5 days

Narrative

John Naberezny played trombone for the Lawrence Symphony Orchestra. Included here are two images from the Lawrence Sunday Sun newspaper from March 11, 1928 of him.

*Marian Thomas (granddaughter) recalls her father (Raymond Naber) discussing that John was very talented and was at one point asked to play with the Boston Pops Orchestra, though he turned it down.

John Naberezy's primary occupation was as a mill operator in Lawrence Massachusetts.
