Narrative

Raymond Naber, a WW2 veteran, spent most of his life living in Franklin Massachusetts where he owned Puritan Drug Company which had a storefront on the main street in town. Ray earned a degree in Pharamacy from Massachusetts College of Pharmacy using the GI Bill.
